At Battery Buddy, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our Battery Buddy application.

Information We Collect

Device Information

  • Battery level and charging status
  • Device model and operating system
  • App usage statistics and performance data

Usage Data

  • App features you use most frequently
  • Notification preferences and settings
  • Error reports and crash data (anonymized)

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide battery monitoring and management services
  • Send you relevant notifications about battery status
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Develop new features based on user needs

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored locally on your device. We do not:

  • Upload your personal data to external servers
  • Share your information with third parties
  • Use your data for advertising purposes
  • Track your location or personal activities

Notifications

Battery Buddy may send you notifications about:

  • Low battery alerts
  • Charging completion reminders
  • Battery health recommendations
  • App updates and new features

You can control notification preferences in your device settings or within the app.

Third-Party Services

Battery Buddy may use the following third-party services:

  • Microsoft Store: For app distribution and updates
  • Flutter Framework: For app development and analytics

These services have their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.

Children's Privacy

Battery Buddy is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@batterybuddy.app
  • Support: support@batterybuddy.app
