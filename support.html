Frequently Asked Questions

How does Battery Buddy monitor my battery?
Battery Buddy uses your device's built-in battery APIs to monitor battery level, charging status, and health. The app runs in the background to provide real-time updates and notifications.
Why am I not receiving notifications?
Make sure Battery Buddy has notification permissions enabled in your device settings. Go to Settings > Apps > Battery Buddy > Notifications and ensure notifications are turned on.
Does Battery Buddy drain my battery?
No, Battery Buddy is designed to be lightweight and efficient. It uses minimal system resources and only monitors battery status when necessary. The app itself consumes less than 1% of your battery.
How accurate are the battery health recommendations?
Our recommendations are based on industry best practices and your device's battery data. However, actual battery performance may vary based on usage patterns and device conditions.
Can I customize notification settings?
Yes! You can customize notification thresholds, timing, and types in the app settings. Choose when you want to be notified about low battery, charging completion, and other events.
Is my data safe with Battery Buddy?
Absolutely! Battery Buddy stores all your data locally on your device. We don't upload or share your personal information. See our Privacy Policy for more details.
